Oncozim by Takwirira

About Us: Empowering Health Through Quantitative Innovation

Oncozim by Takwirira is a health technology initiative focused on advancing cancer care and medical research in Zimbabwe through rigorous quantitative methods and intelligent automation. We develop advanced, automated AI systems that help clinicians detect and measure tumors from radiological imaging with unparalleled speed and precision. Our open and transparent pipelines, built on robust data science principles, accelerate diagnostics and support impactful research for better patient outcomes.

Founded by Khumbula (Takwirira, Barachel), a Zimbabwean PhD candidate in Physics at the University of New Hampshire (starting Fall 2025), Oncozim is driven by a personal commitment to expanding access to quality cancer care. With a background rooted in complex problem-solving and data analysis, Takwirira understands the critical need for scalable, data-driven solutions in healthcare. This initiative leverages advanced statistical modeling and machine learning algorithms to transform care through innovation and capacity building.

Our vision is to forge lasting partnerships with hospitals and research institutions across Zimbabwe, using cutting-edge technology and quantitative expertise to transform oncology and empower local healthcare systems.

Our Services

AI-Powered Tumor Detection & Quantification

Instant analysis of medical scan images (DICOM) to assess cancer risk and quantify tumor progression using advanced, expertly trained AI models. This enables earlier and more accurate diagnoses, providing actionable data points for clinical decision-making.

Clinical Research Pipeline Tools

Command-line tools and robust APIs empower clinical researchers to efficiently process and analyze large imaging and patient datasets at scale. This fosters local research, enabling data-driven insights and contributions to global medical knowledge.

Data Integration & Interoperability

Robust support for structured CSV metadata, clinical tagging, and export-ready research formats. Our solutions seamlessly integrate with existing hospital systems, enhancing healthcare workflows and ensuring data consistency and accessibility in Zimbabwe.

Quantitative Health Education

Empowering communities through knowledge. Interactive quizzes present essential oncology facts with 10 new shuffled questions weekly. We aim to translate this content into local languages to reach all of Zimbabwe, fostering data literacy and informed health decisions.

Imaging & Quantitative Health Quiz

Medical imaging techniques like MRI, CT, and PET are essential for early cancer detection and treatment. However, many Zimbabweans lack access to and understanding of these tools.

Early diagnosis improves treatment success and survival, especially in cancers common in Zimbabwe, where late detection remains a challenge.

Oncozim by Takwirira develops AI-driven software to detect tumors directly from medical images, enabling faster, more accessible diagnoses and better patient outcomes, leveraging quantitative image analysis and predictive modeling.

Being informed empowers patients to engage confidently with their care teams and make better health decisions, fostering data literacy in public health.

Take our quiz to learn key facts about cancer imaging. Questions refresh weekly, and we plan to offer translations to reach all communities in Zimbabwe. This quiz is designed to test and improve quantitative understanding of medical imaging principles.
